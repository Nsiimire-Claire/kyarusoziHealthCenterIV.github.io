Quality Healthcare Services

Kyarusozi Health Centre IV is a Ugandan health facility located in Kyenjojo District, Kyarusozi Town Council, Kyarusozi Ward, Kyamugenyi Cell. We are committed to providing accessible and quality healthcare services to our community and neighboring areas.


Our dedicated team of healthcare professionals ensures that each patient receives the necessary care and attention they deserve. We offer a wide range of services, including patient registration, triage, evaluation, laboratory testing, prescription and medication dispensing, inpatient care, maternity care, pediatric care, and general medical services.

PATIENT INFORMATION MANAGEMENT

Paper-Based System

Currently, Kyarusozi Health Centre IV relies on a paper-based information management system. When patients arrive at the facility, their information is recorded by the registration clerk, including their name, next of kin, age, weight, and nutritional status. Patients are provided with a patient's book, which they carry with them to different areas within the facility. The healthcare providers record assessments, prescriptions, and treatment details in the patient's book.
