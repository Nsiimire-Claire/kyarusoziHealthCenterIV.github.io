Facilities and Amenities

Outpatient Services

Our outpatient services cater to the healthcare needs of individuals who do not require overnight hospitalization. We provide diagnosis, treatment, and medical advice for a wide range of medical conditions.

Services Offered:

- Consultation with specialist doctors
- Diagnostic tests and imaging services
- Medication dispensing
- Vaccinations and immunizations
- Health screenings and check-ups
- Family planning services
- Counseling and mental health support
- Referrals to specialist care when needed

Our outpatient department is equipped with modern medical equipment and staffed by experienced healthcare professionals dedicated to providing quality care to our patients.

8.5 Excellent

Inpatient Services

Our inpatient services provide comprehensive care for patients who require overnight hospitalization for medical treatment, observation, or surgical procedures. We ensure that our patients receive personalized care in a comfortable and safe environment.

Services Offered:

- Hospitalization and medical observation
- Surgical procedures and post-operative care
- Intensive care and critical care services
- Maternity and neonatal care
- Pediatrics and child healthcare
- Chronic disease management
- Rehabilitation services

Our inpatient wards are staffed by a multidisciplinary team of healthcare professionals, including doctors, nurses, and support staff, who work together to provide holistic care to our patients.

9.0 Superb

Diagnostic and Laboratory Services

Our diagnostic and laboratory services play a crucial role in the diagnosis, monitoring, and treatment of medical conditions. We utilize advanced medical technology and techniques to provide accurate and timely test results for our patients.

Services Offered:

- Blood tests and hematology
- Urine and stool analysis
- Imaging services (X-ray, ultrasound, CT scan)
- ECG and cardiac monitoring
- Microbiology and infectious disease testing
- Histopathology and cytology
- Genetic testing and molecular diagnostics
- Specialized tests for various medical conditions

Our diagnostic and laboratory services are accredited and staffed by certified medical technologists and technicians who ensure the highest standards of quality and safety.

9.5 Exceptional
