About Us

INTRODUCTION:
Kyarusozi Health Center IV is a government aided health facility located in Kyenjojo District, Kyarusozi Town Council. It started operating as a health center II many years ago and later was upgraded to a health center III due to increasing demands for its services by the local people. Currently, Kyarusozi Health Center III has further been promoted to a health center IV to meet the continuously increasing demand for healthcare services and based on the Community Health Needs Assessment. Kyarusozi Health Center IV aims to provide quality healthcare service delivery to all people within the surrounding community with equality and fairness from all backgrounds.

BACKGROUND:
Since its inception, Kyarusozi Health Center IV has been a cornerstone of the local community, providing comprehensive medical services, cutting-edge treatments, and compassionate care to patients from all walks of life. Founded on the principles of integrity, innovation, and excellence, the hospital has consistently adapted to meet the evolving needs of its patients and the broader healthcare landscape.
